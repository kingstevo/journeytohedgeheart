Journey to Hedgeheart

Embark on one snuggly hedgehog's journey to find love! Jump and duck past dancing crabs, laughing birds, spiky cacti, and wobbly flamingos. Watch the "hedgeheart" timer get closer with each obstacle you pass. When the time reaches zero, it's time for love!

Use the up arrow or space bar to jump, and the left and right arrow keys or on-screen controls for mobile devices to move.
